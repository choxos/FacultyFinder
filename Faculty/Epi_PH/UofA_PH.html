Territorial Acknowledgement

The University of Alberta, its buildings, labs and research stations are primarily located on the territory of the Nhiyaw (Cree), Niitsitapi (Blackfoot), Mtis, Nakoda (Stoney), Dene, Haudenosaunee (Iroquois) and Anishinaabe (Ojibway/Saulteaux), lands that are now known as part of Treaties 6, 7 and 8 and homeland of the Mtis. The University of Alberta respects the sovereignty, lands, histories, languages, knowledge systems and cultures of all First Nations, Mtis and Inuit nations.
