About FacultyFinder

FacultyFinder is a comprehensive platform designed to help prospective graduate students and researchers discover faculty members across Canadian universities who align with their research interests and academic goals.

Key Features:
  • Faculty Discovery: Search and filter faculty by research areas, publications, and expertise
  • AI-Powered Matching: Upload your CV for personalized faculty recommendations
  • Publication Analytics: Analyze faculty research impact and collaboration networks
  • University Comparison: Compare institutions and departments across Canada
Data Sources:
  • University faculty directories and profiles
  • PubMed publication database
  • Scimago Journal & Country Rank
  • Academic social media profiles
