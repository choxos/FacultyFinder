About FacultyFinder

Connecting researchers with their ideal academic collaborators worldwide through AI-powered matching and comprehensive faculty profiles.

Our Mission

To democratize academic collaboration by making it easy for researchers, students, and institutions to discover and connect with faculty members whose expertise aligns with their research interests and career goals.

Discover

Find faculty members across universities worldwide with advanced search and filtering capabilities.

Connect

Build meaningful academic relationships through detailed profiles and contact information.

Collaborate

Foster innovation through strategic academic partnerships and research collaborations.

What Makes Us Different

AI-Powered Matching

Our advanced AI analyzes CVs and research interests to provide personalized faculty recommendations, saving researchers time and improving match quality.

Global Coverage

Access faculty information from top universities worldwide, breaking down geographical barriers in academic collaboration.

Comprehensive Metrics

View detailed publication records, citation metrics, collaboration networks, and research impact data to make informed decisions.

Reliable Data

Our database is regularly updated from official university sources and academic databases to ensure accuracy and completeness.

Our Data Sources

University Websites

Official faculty directories and department pages from partner universities worldwide.

Academic Databases

PubMed, ORCID, Google Scholar, and other reputable academic publication databases.

Journal Rankings

Scimago Journal & Country Rank data for comprehensive publication quality metrics.
